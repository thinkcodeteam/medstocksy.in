Who We Are

MedStocksy is a cloud-based pharmacy inventory management system and customer relationship management (CRM) platform designed specifically for pharmacies, medical stores, and healthcare retailers across India.

Our Vision

We empower Indian pharmacies with smart technology that simplifies inventory management, strengthens customer relationships, and drives sustainable business growth.

Our Mission

To empower Indian pharmacies with smart technology that simplifies inventory management, strengthens customer relationships, and drives business growth.

By automating manual processes and providing real-time insights, we help pharmacy owners save time, reduce errors, prevent revenue loss, and focus on what matters most: serving their patients better.

What Makes MedStocksy Different

Unlike generic inventory management software, MedStocksy is purpose-built for pharmacies. Every feature, every workflow, and every decision we make considers the specific needs of pharmacy operations.

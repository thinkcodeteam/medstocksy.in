Medstocksy vs. Medstocks: What's the Difference?

Feb 08, 2026 • 8 min read • Product Comparison

Indian pharmacists often ask: "Isn't Medstocksy the same as Medstocks?" The honest answer is no—they serve completely different purposes. Confusing them would be like comparing a restaurant POS system to a food delivery platform. Let me break down the key differences so you make the right choice for your pharmacy.

Quick Verdict

Medstocksy is an Operating System for retail pharmacies. Medstocks is a Bulk Ordering Platform for distributors.

Common Questions

Q: Can I use both Medstocksy and Medstocks together?

Yes! Many larger pharmacy chains use Medstocks for B2B bulk ordering and Medstocksy for their retail shop management. They serve different supply-chain roles.

Q: Is Medstocksy only for large pharmacy chains?

No. Small independent pharmacies with 2-3 staff members are our target. The system is designed to be simple with zero IT knowledge required.

Q: Why does Google suggest Medstocks when I search for pharmacy software?

Medstocks has been around longer and invests heavily in ads. But for retail pharmacy management, Medstocksy's features are far more comprehensive.
